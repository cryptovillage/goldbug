MADAME REED'S HOUSE OF THE UNDEAD

Piano

"AAARRGHHH! DON'T OPEN THAT!" Madame Reed shrieks. Before she can stop you, the lid covering the piano keys is lifted by an unseen force.

"I have been trying to silence the piano; it has been driving me batty. It will not stop playing the same two notes over and over again. It will continue as long as you leave it open."

The eerie, shrill notes ring through the air.
